DATA FLOW:
User types /approval-request
    ↓
socket_handler.go receives slash command
    ↓
Opens modal with form fields
    ↓
User fills form and submits
    ↓
socket_handler.go extracts form data
    ↓
approval_service.go creates approval record
    ↓
Saves to database (models.ApprovalRequest)
    ↓
Sends rich message to Slack channel
    ↓
Message shows with Approve/Reject buttons

EVENT FLOW:
Slack sends event
    ↓
Start() receives socketmode.Event
    ↓
Check evt.Type (socketmode.EventType)
    ├─ EventTypeSlashCommand → handleSlashCommand()
    ├─ EventTypeInteractive → handleInteractive()
    └─ EventTypeConnected → log message

If EventTypeInteractive:
    ↓
handleInteractive() checks callback.Type (slack.InteractionType)
    ├─ InteractionTypeBlockActions → handleBlockAction()
    └─ InteractionTypeViewSubmission → handleModalSubmission()

1. Frontend → API (POST /api/v1/approval/request)
2. API → Kafka (approval.requested)
3. Approval Service Consumer → Processes request
4. Approval Service → Slack (sends message)
5. Human → Slack (clicks Approve/Reject)
6. Slack → Approval Service (interaction callback)
7. Approval Service → Kafka (approval.completed)
8. Business Consumer → Processes completion
9. Business Consumer → Kafka (action.executed OR action.rejected)
10. Frontend → Listens to action topics

BUSINESS CONSUMER SERVICE:
- File: service/v1/business_consumer.go
- Consumes: approval.completed topic
- Publishes: action.executed (when approved) or action.rejected (when rejected)
- Consumer Group: business-consumer-group
- Message Type: resources.ActionMessage

KAFKA TOPICS:
1. approval.requested - Published by API, consumed by Approval Service
2. approval.completed - Published by Approval Service, consumed by Business Consumer
3. action.executed - Published by Business Consumer when approved
4. action.rejected - Published by Business Consumer when rejected
